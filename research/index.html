Research

A brief overview of my scientific research at the University of Bristol. I am always open to new collaborations and research visits, from PhD students through to Professors and Researchers in Industry. If you interested in working with me, see the new collaborations page below.

Background

I focus on the application of scientific machine learning (SciML), particularly physics-based machine learning, to nonlinear dynamics problems coming from engineering. I also have a broad range of research interests across the field of applied mathematics and computer science. I focus particularly on engineering-related applications (e.g., aeroelastic flutter) and I have a sideline in tactile robotics (joint with Nathan Lepora).
